Rules — Strict

Read these before chatting. Keep the community clean, respectful, and easy for everyone to enjoy.

Respect Everyone

Keep It PG

This is a family-friendly chat. Avoid explicit language, NSFW content, or anything inappropriate.

No Spamming

Keep chat clean and on-topic. Do not spam messages, emojis, links, or repeated copy-paste posts.

Follow Channel Topics

Keep discussion relevant to the stream, the current topic, or the community space being used.

No Self-Promotion or Advertising

No unsolicited advertising, self-promotion, or external links without permission.

Use Voice Channels Responsibly

Be respectful in voice spaces. Do not disrupt conversations with loud noises, music, trolling, or interruptions.

Follow Twitch Terms of Service

No Sharing Personal Information

Do not share personal information about others without explicit consent, including names, addresses, contact info, or private details.

Moderator Decisions Are Final

Enjoy Your Stay

No Legionnaire or Anonymous Mentions

No mentions of Legionnaires or Anonymous whatsoever. This rule is final. Mentions may result in timeouts. After 3 timeouts in the same month, the user may be banned from Twitch chat.

The Ultimate Rule

Do not make us have to come back and add more rules. Use common sense and courteous judgment in all interactions.
